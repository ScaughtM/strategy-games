The Game

Your Tutorial Opponents

The Rules of the Game

- Ronald Coase

"This is the game of firms and markets. You will compete for dominance of an abstract commodities market. Here you have an opportunity to demonstrate your mastery of Strategy. Pay close attention to this guide, it'll help you understand each individual phase of the game."

Phase 1 - Research and Development

- David Ricardo

"In the first phase, you will given the opportunity to invest funds into Research. The base cost for investing stays consistent, but the potential rewards vary! A strong investment result can launch your firm to dominance. Remember, a lower cost function is better than a higher one!"

Phase 2 - Competition

- Frank Knight

"After finishing the first Phase, you will be given a choice to compete in the market for that round. The more successful your research investments, the more successful, and profitable, you will be. Remember that it might sometimes be more wise to wait to compete."

Phase 3 - Round Results

- Alfred Marshall

"In this phase, you have an opportunity to take in the lay of the land and evaluate how successful you've been relative to your competitors. In this phase you will see how much revenue you generated that round as well as your relative position"

Phase 4 - After the round ends

- Freidrich Hayek

"After the results phase, you'll either begin a new round or, if the game has ended, you'll see the game results. In this first game, you'll be playing us, and you will play 6 rounds. In other games, you may play other user characters and the games may be longer... Good Luck!"
